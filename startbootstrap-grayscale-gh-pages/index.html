Momma's life story

Momma dog was originally named "Faith". She lived in Redding, California with an amazing family. She was a lover and slept with the kids. She treated as them her own, even though she had never had puppies at the time and was still a puppy herself.

We were looking for another puppy. Specifically a companion for our male American Bully named, Turbo. We drove all the way down from Eastern Washington to get Momma Dog. She instantly loved us and welcomed us as her new family into her heart.

She took on the new name "Momma Dog" about a year after we got her. She was pregnant with 12 large healthy puppies. Ever since we started calling her "Momma dog" she would get happy as if she knew what we were saying. We rarley ever use her 'birth' name, "Faith" unless she is in trouble or if we need to get her attention quickly.

Momma Dog is an American Shaffordshire Terrier , she is loving, a sunggle bug, and has a huge heart. Many folk may think that she is scary looking because of her breed, but I think she is just adorable!
